Mahan Jung



"Born and raised in Pokhara valley overlooking massive Annapurna Range as a child I used to draw Mt. Fishtail (Macchapuchre) whenever I’ve to draw something in my classroom. From the beginning these mountains had stamped a different impact on me. Growing in Pokhara is the luckiest thing I believe as we are surrounded by lakes, green hills and massive ranges of mountain. My love towards nature is from the very beginning when I started to see and understand. As I was growing I kept travelling and exploring around the valley which allows me to understand and feel nature more.

Gradually, I did graduation in Tourism Management as I was always curious about traveling and exploring outdoors. After graduation I kept exploring more about mountaineering, since then it’s been a hell of a journey for becoming Snowboard instructor to Mountain/Trekking Guide. But still long way to go, so much to learn and so much to see. Staying raw and curious is my main strength."
